Introduction

We see world models appear everywhere from robotics to self driving cars. These are seen as the answer to many of the shortcommings of standard modeling approaches. The size of the models necessary to process information from the camera setups on robots and self driving cars can be cost prohobitive. I still wanted to play with the idea of world models though. To that end I devised a very simply ball world, in this world the ball bounces around aimlesly for N frames. What I want is for the model to take as inpu the preceding frames and output the next frame in essence predicting where the ball would go next. It is an extremely simple world to model but it does incorporate the necessary pieces of more complete world models.
